========================================================================
                   REELSCRIPT 1.0 - COMPLETE USER MANUAL
========================================================================

Welcome to ReelScript! This guide covers everything you need to know to 
write, edit, collaborate, and use the built-in AI assistant. 

Please read through this manual, especially the "Collaboration & Revisions" 
section, to ensure smooth file sharing.


========================================================================
 1. INSTALLATION & FIRST STEPS
========================================================================

1. INSTALLING THE APP:
   - Right-click the "install.bat" file and select "Run as Administrator".
   - This is required so Windows can associate the new ".rsp" (ReelScript 
     Project) files with the application.
   - Once installed, you will find a ReelScript shortcut on your Desktop.

2. SET UP YOUR AUTHOR PROFILE (CRUCIAL):
   Before you start editing, you need to tell ReelScript who you are so 
   your edits are properly tagged.
   - Open ReelScript.
   - Click "Customize" in the top menu -> "Author Profile & Revision Color".
   - Type in your Name.
   - Click the color box to pick a specific color for your edits (e.g., 
     Blue, Purple, Green). 
   - Click "Save Profile". 


========================================================================
 2. COLLABORATION & REVISIONS (HOW WE WORK TOGETHER)
========================================================================

We will be trading a single ".rsp" (ReelScript Project) file back and forth. 
To make sure we don't accidentally overwrite each other's work without 
noticing, ReelScript has a built-in Revision Tracker.

TURNING ON REVISION TRACKING:
Whenever you sit down to make edits to my script:
1. Click "Revisions" in the top menu.
2. Click "Track Revisions".
3. A checkmark will appear next to it indicating it is ON.

WHAT HAPPENS WHEN TRACKING IS ON?
Any time you change a word, delete a sentence, or add a new line, ReelScript 
will automatically place a colored asterisk (*) next to that line. 
- The asterisk will be in the color you picked in your Author Profile.
- If I hover my mouse over your asterisk, a tooltip will pop up showing 
  your name, so I know exactly who made the change.

LEAVING REVISION NOTES:
If you want to explain WHY you changed something, or leave a comment on a 
specific line without changing the script itself:
1. Right-click the colored asterisk (*) next to the line.
2. Select "📝 Note" from the menu.
3. ReelScript will automatically copy the line, attach your name, and jump 
   you to the "Revision Notes" document where you can type your thoughts. 
   I can read this document when I open the file.

APPROVING CHANGES:
If I make a change and you want to "Approve" it (removing my asterisk so 
it becomes a permanent, normal part of the script):
1. Right-click my asterisk.
2. Select "✅ Approve". The asterisk will vanish.

HOW TO GET YOUR EDITS BACK TO ME:
1. When you are done editing, click "File" -> "Save Project".
2. Click "Share" in the top menu -> "Open Shared Google Folder".
3. This will open our shared Google Drive folder in your web browser.
4. Drag and drop the saved ".rsp" file from your computer into that Google 
   Drive folder to overwrite the old one.


========================================================================
 3. THE INTERFACE & WRITING
========================================================================

THE LEFT SIDEBAR:
On the far left, you will see three main icons:
- 📋 Script: Shows your Project Documents (Default Document, Revision Notes, 
  and Title Page). Click these to switch between them.
- 🎬 Scenes: A dynamically generated list of every Scene Heading in the 
  script. Click a scene to instantly jump to it.
- 💬 My Notes: A PRIVATE scratchpad. Anything you type here stays strictly 
  on your computer. It does NOT save into the shared project file. Use this 
  for your own personal brainstorming.

SMART FORMATTING (TAB & ENTER):
You do not need to click formatting buttons while typing. The editor knows 
standard screenplay formatting:
- If you are on a Scene Heading and press Enter, it drops to Action.
- If you are on Action and press Tab, it jumps to Character.
- If you type a Character and press Enter, it jumps to Dialogue.
- If you are in Dialogue and press Enter, it jumps back to Character.
- Pressing Enter on an empty Action line jumps to Character.
- Pressing Enter in the middle of a sentence perfectly splits the text in two.

HOTKEYS:
Ctrl+1 = Scene Heading      Ctrl+5 = Dialogue
Ctrl+2 = Action             Ctrl+6 = Transition
Ctrl+3 = Character          Ctrl+7 = Shot
Ctrl+4 = Parenthetical      Ctrl+Enter = Hard Page Break


========================================================================
 4. THE AI ASSISTANT
========================================================================

ReelScript is connected directly to Google's Gemini AI to act as a 
co-writer and developmental editor.

1. GETTING AI SUGGESTIONS (Brainstorming)
   - Highlight a line of dialogue, an action line, or a whole paragraph.
   - Click the "💡 Suggest" button in the top toolbar (or Right-Click -> 
     Get AI Suggestion).
   - A draggable window will appear with ideas, rewrites, and suggestions 
     based on the text you highlighted.

2. AI AUTO-FIX (Grammar & Flow)
   - Highlight a clunky sentence.
   - Right-click and select "✨ AI Auto-Fix Selection".
   - The AI will rewrite the sentence for better flow and perfect grammar 
     right before your eyes.

3. SCENE AI ANALYSIS
   - Want feedback on just one specific scene?
   - Open the "🎬 Scenes" sidebar on the left.
   - Right-click the scene you want feedback on -> "🎬 AI Check Scene" 
     (Or put your cursor in the scene and click "🎬 Scene AI" in the toolbar).
   - The AI will read the scene and generate a detailed report on pacing, 
     dialogue, and formatting, referencing specific line numbers.

4. FULL SCRIPT ANALYSIS
   - Click "Reports" in the top menu -> "🤖 AI Script Analysis".
   - The AI will read the entire script and give you a comprehensive 
     developmental edit. (This takes about 20-30 seconds).
   - Line numbers in the AI reports are clickable! Click "Line#45" in the 
     report, and the editor will instantly scroll to that exact line and 
     highlight it for you.


========================================================================
 5. BACKUPS & AUTO-SAVE (NEVER LOSE WORK)
========================================================================

ReelScript has a hyper-aggressive backup system. To configure it:
Click "File" -> "Manage Backups & Auto-Save" (or Ctrl+Shift+S).

1. AUTO-SAVE INTERVAL:
   Choose how often the app saves in the background (default is 5 mins). 
   It also auto-saves every time you pause typing for 1 second.

2. LOCAL BACKUP FOLDER:
   By default, backups are saved to your "Documents/ReelScript" folder.

3. CLOUD BACKUP FOLDER (Highly Recommended):
   You can select a secondary folder (like your personal Google Drive or 
   Dropbox folder) for ReelScript to save duplicate backups into.

4. MAX BACKUP LIMIT:
   To prevent eating up hard drive space, ReelScript limits backups to 5 
   per folder. When it saves the 6th backup, it silently deletes the oldest 
   one. You can change this limit in the Backup menu.

MANUAL SNAPSHOTS:
If you are about to rewrite a huge section and want to save a permanent 
"save state" you can revert to if you mess up:
- Click "Revisions" -> "Manage Snapshots".
- Type a name (e.g., "Before the big rewrite") and click "Create Snapshot".
- You can restore this exact state at any time.


========================================================================
 6. USEFUL TOOLS & FEATURES
========================================================================

CHARACTER FILTER & RENAME:
- Tools -> Character Filter: Hides the entire script except for the 
  dialogue of the characters you select. Great for reading a character's 
  arc back-to-back.
- Tools -> Rename Character: Globally renames a character's headers AND 
  smart-replaces their name anywhere it appears in dialogue/action lines.

FIND & REPLACE:
- Edit -> Find & Replace (Ctrl+F). Standard search tool with Match Case 
  and Replace All options.

SPELLCHECK:
- Tools -> Check Spelling. 
- ReelScript uses a custom dictionary. It ignores standard screenplay 
  abbreviations (INT/EXT) but will catch typos. 
- Click "Add to Dict" to teach it custom sci-fi/fantasy names so it stops 
  flagging them.

FOCUS & DARK MODE:
- View -> Toggle Focus Mode (F11): Hides all menus and UI elements so you 
  can just write.
- Customize -> Dark Mode: Switches the white page to a dark slate gray to 
  save your eyes at night.

EXPORTING:
- File -> Export PDF: Generates a perfectly formatted, industry-standard 
  PDF, including your Title Page.
- File -> Export Final Draft (.fdx): If you ever need to send the script 
  to a producer or agent who demands Final Draft format, this will generate 
  a perfect native .fdx file.

Happy Writing!
